# Account and Data Deletion

## What data is tracked after you log in?

- **Wallet Address**
- **Logged In Email**
- **IP Address**
- **Operating System**
- **Device**
- **Interaction with the Platform**

## How can I delete my account?

Once you log out, any data associated with your personal account stops being tracked. Any personal data associated with your account is automatically deleted after 15 days of inactivity.
