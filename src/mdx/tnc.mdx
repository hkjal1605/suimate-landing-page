Nexa is an advanced on-chain data analytics and trading platform designed to provide users with real-time insights into blockchain activities.

The services offered by the Platform (the "Services") include the following:

- **Data and Analytics:** The Platform provides on-chain data analytics services, allowing users to view activities on-chain from any address, view price charts, market cap, liquidity, past trades, and other related data of cryptocurrencies and tokens launched on the Sui blockchain.

  - **Direct Trade:** You can buy or sell coins directly from your wallet. This requires you to approve each transaction from your wallet.
  - **Deposit to Trading Account:** You can deposit coins into your Nexa Trading Account. This option enables the "One-click trades" feature, where trades can be made without requiring wallet approval for each transaction.

- **Copy Trading:** Users can copy trades from other wallets, set up trade alerts, view trade history, and assess the past performance of other traders on the Platform.
- **Liquidity Management:** Users can view and interact with liquidity positions deployed on other platforms, such as adding, removing, or modifying liquidity positions from the Platform.
- **Memezone:** The Memezone page displays coins created on meme-based or pump-fun platforms within the Sui ecosystem. This section provides an overview of such coins, and users can track their performance.

## TRADE DETAILS

Trade details include the include the following parameters:

- **Trade Slippage:** The potential difference between the expected and actual value of input and output coins due to market fluctuations.
- **Buy Price:** The price at which the trade will be executed at the time of order placement.
- **MEV Protection:** Nexa integrates MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) protection, powered by third-party service provider Shio, to safeguard users against front-running and sandwich attacks on the Sui blockchain. Users can opt in or out of this feature at their discretion.
- **Max Slippage:** Users may set a maximum allowable slippage threshold, beyond which the transaction will be automatically canceled.
- **Priority Fee:** Users may elect to pay an additional gas fee to expedite the processing of their transaction.
- **Trade Fee:** A 1% fee is charged by Nexa on the trade size, which will be deducted from the trade amount.
